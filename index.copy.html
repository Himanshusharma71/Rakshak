Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to provide a tool that helps women assess their safety in any location. We understand that personal safety is a fundamental concern, and we want to make it easier for everyone to take proactive steps towards their well-being. Our website allows you to input crucial information like your age, BMI (Body Mass Index), and area number. Using advanced machine learning models, we analyze this data and provide you with valuable insights on whether a particular location is safe for you or not. Your safety is our top priority, and our platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all.
